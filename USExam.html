U.S. Amateur Radio License Exam

In Association with The GLAARG (Greater Los Angeles Amateur Radio Group) VEC is one of the 14 FCC-recognized Volunteer-Exam Coordinators conducting amateur radio exams. GLAARG is a VEC testing organization, not a radio club. Satara Institute of Hams conducts USA Amateur Radio exam on regular basis. SIH has own VEs (Volunteer Examiner's) team for US examination in India.

  • Remotely Administered Exam Sessions - Online
  • Offline Exam sessions

    • Learn More

Eligibility

GLAARG VEC INDIA VE TEAM will hold a FCC's Amateur Radio License exam session. Technician, General and Amateur Extra Class license exams will be given. You must provide a mailing address in the U.S., and a FCC Registration Number (FRN) before taking the exam. Pre-Registration is required for the exam.

Requirements for Exam:

1. Any Indian Govt issued Photo ID proof.
2. USA mailing address
3. FRN (FCC Registration Number)
4. Computer with webcam & high speed internet (For Online Exam Only)
